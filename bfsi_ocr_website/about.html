About BFSI OCR System

Welcome to the BFSI OCR (Banking, Financial Services, and Insurance Optical Character Recognition) System. Our platform aims to provide automated, efficient, and accurate document processing solutions for BFSI sectors, enabling faster loan approvals and detailed document analysis using AI and OCR technology.

Key Features:

  • OCR-based Text Extraction:
    Extracts data from various document formats such as PDFs, images, and Word files.
  • Loan Approval System:
    AI-driven loan eligibility prediction for students and other applicants based on their financial data.
  • Advanced Analytics:
    Visualizations such as bar, pie, and line charts for loan and transaction data analysis.
  • User Authentication:
    Secure authentication with OTP-based login and data protection.
  • Multi-platform Support:
    Easy deployment options on AWS, GCP, and Azure for scalability.

Our Vision:

We strive to revolutionize the BFSI sector by leveraging cutting-edge AI and OCR technologies to enhance efficiency, transparency, and decision-making processes in financial services.
